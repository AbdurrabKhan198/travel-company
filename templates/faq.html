Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, simply search for your desired route and travel dates on our homepage. Select your preferred flight, enter passenger details, review your booking, and proceed to payment. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a booking confirmation email with your ticket details.

Can I book for multiple passengers?

Yes, you can book for multiple passengers in a single booking. During the booking process, you can add multiple passengers and enter their details. For group bookings of 10 or more passengers, please use our Group Request feature for special pricing and arrangements.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

After successful payment, you will receive a booking confirmation email with your booking reference number. You can also check your booking status in the "My Trips" section of your dashboard. A confirmed booking will show "Confirmed" status.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Booking modifications depend on the airline's policy and fare type. Some bookings can be modified (subject to change fees), while others may be non-changeable. Please contact our customer support or check your booking details for modification options. Change fees and fare differences may apply.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallet payments (Cash Balance Wallet and OD Wallet). All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway partners.

Are the prices shown final?

Prices are subject to change until payment is confirmed. The final price will be displayed before you proceed to payment. All prices include applicable taxes and fees unless stated otherwise.

What is Cash Balance Wallet?

Cash Balance Wallet is available to all registered users. You can recharge it yourself and use the balance for bookings. It's like a prepaid wallet that makes booking faster and easier. You can view your balance and transaction history anytime.

What is OD Wallet?

OD (Organizational Discount) Wallet is a special wallet that can only be activated and recharged by admin. It's designed for corporate clients or special arrangements. Contact admin to activate your OD Wallet.

Cancellation & Refunds

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking from the "My Trips" section in your dashboard. Click on the booking you want to cancel and select "Cancel Booking". Please note that cancellation charges apply as per the airline's policy and fare type. Some bookings may be non-refundable.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 7-14 business days after cancellation approval. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used. If you paid via wallet, the refund will be credited to your wallet balance.

Are cancellation charges refundable?

No, cancellation charges and service fees are non-refundable. These charges are set by the airline and are deducted from your refund amount. The remaining amount after deducting cancellation charges will be refunded to you.

Travel Documents & Visa

Do I need a visa for international travel?

Visa requirements depend on your destination and nationality. Some countries offer visa-free entry or visa-on-arrival for Indian passport holders. We recommend checking visa requirements for your destination well in advance. You can use our Visit Visa service for visa assistance.

What documents do I need to travel?

For domestic travel, a valid government-issued ID (Aadhar, Passport, Driving License) is required. For international travel, you need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), visa (if required), and any other documents specified by the destination country. Always carry your booking confirmation and e-ticket.

How can I apply for a visa through your website?

Visit our "Visit Visa" page, select your destination country, and fill out the visa application form. Submit the required documents and pay the visa processing fee. Our team will process your application and guide you through the process. Processing times vary by destination.

Travel Packages

How do I apply for a travel package?

Browse our popular packages on the homepage, click on "Apply Now" for your desired destination, fill out the application form with your travel details and requirements, and submit. Our team will contact you with a customized package quote and further details.

Can I customize a package?

Yes, you can customize packages according to your preferences. Mention your requirements in the "Special Requests" section when applying, or contact our team directly. We'll create a personalized itinerary that suits your needs and budget.

Account & Support

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign Up" in the top navigation, enter your email address, create a password, and complete the registration form. You'll receive a verification email. After verification, your account will be activated and you can start booking.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us through our "Contact" page, email us at support@safarzometravels.com, or call our customer support number. We're available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST. For urgent travel-related issues, please call our 24/7 emergency helpline.

Where can I view my booking history?

Log in to your account and go to "My Trips" or "Dashboard" section. Here you can view all your bookings, including upcoming trips, past trips, and cancelled bookings. You can also download tickets and view booking details.
